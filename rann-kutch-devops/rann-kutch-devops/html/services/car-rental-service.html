Car Rental Tips

  • Inspect the car thoroughly before accepting it – check for scratches, dents, and photograph any existing damage
  • Verify that spare tire, jack, toolkit, and all documents (RC, insurance) are in the vehicle
  • Test AC, music system, and all features before leaving the rental office
  • Keep emergency contact numbers saved and roadside assistance details handy
  • Follow all traffic rules – challans are deducted from your security deposit
  • Refuel from authorized pumps and keep fuel receipts for your records
  • Return the car on time to avoid late return penalties (₹500/hour)
  • Clean the interior before returning – excessive dirtiness may incur cleaning charges
  • Take photos/video of the car during return as proof of condition
  • Get the final settlement receipt with deposit refund confirmation in writing
