What is Outstation Taxi Service?

An outstation taxi service is designed for travelers who need a dedicated vehicle for multiple days or extended journeys covering multiple destinations. Unlike airport transfers or local city rides, outstation taxis are booked for trips that extend beyond city limits and often include overnight stays. The service typically includes a driver who stays with you throughout the journey, providing both transportation and local knowledge.

For Rann of Kutch exploration, an outstation taxi is particularly valuable because the region is vast and attractions are spread across considerable distances. You might want to visit the White Rann, explore Bhuj's historical sites, relax at Mandvi Beach, shop for Kutchi handicrafts, visit ancient temples, and experience local villages – all of which requires flexible, reliable transportation across multiple days.

Planning Your Outstation Trip to Kutch

  • Book 1–2 weeks in advance during Oct–Mar to ensure availability
  • Carry cash; many places don’t accept cards
  • Pack warm nights/cool days clothing—temperatures vary
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, hat—desert sun can be intense
  • Download offline maps—connectivity is patchy
  • Share dietary needs with driver ahead of time
  • Carry first-aid/meds; facilities are limited rurally
  • Respect local customs; ask before photographing people/sites
  • Stay hydrated; carry bottled water
  • Inform about mobility needs in advance
