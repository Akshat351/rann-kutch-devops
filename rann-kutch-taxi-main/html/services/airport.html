Airport Transfer Tips

  • Book your airport transfer at least 24 hours in advance to ensure vehicle availability
  • Provide accurate flight details including flight number and arrival time for proper tracking
  • Save the driver's contact number immediately – useful for any last-minute coordination
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible for driver communication upon landing
  • Clear customs and baggage claim as quickly as possible to meet driver within free waiting time
  • If you can't find the driver, call immediately rather than waiting at the wrong location
  • Verify the driver's identity by checking their photo and vehicle number before boarding
  • For international arrivals, exchange some currency at airport for toll/parking payments
  • Inform the driver about any stops needed (ATM, pharmacy, etc.) before starting journey
  • Keep the booking confirmation handy – it has all important details and support contact
