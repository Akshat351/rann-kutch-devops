Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers

How do I book a taxi?

Simply fill out the booking form above with your travel details, or contact us directly via phone or WhatsApp. We'll confirm your booking within 30 minutes.

What is the cancellation policy?

Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a small fee. Contact us for specific details.

Are your drivers experienced?

Yes, all our drivers are licensed, experienced, and familiar with Gujarat routes. They undergo regular training and background verification.

Do you provide night service?

Absolutely! We operate 24/7 with no extra night charges. Just mention your preferred pickup time in the booking form.

Can I modify my booking?

Yes, you can modify your booking by contacting us at least 6 hours before the scheduled pickup time. We'll do our best to accommodate your changes.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, UPI, credit/debit cards, and online bank transfers. Payment can be made after the trip or in advance as per your preference.
